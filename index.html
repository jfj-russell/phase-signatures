This page contains animated figures for my project "Quantum Tunnelling Without a Barrier: Phase-Sensitive Signatures in HHG". The varied quantity in all figures is the harmonic of the emitted photon q. I tried to make these interactive but I couldn't get it to work, so if anybody knows how to do that in julia pls let me know.
