This page contains animated figures from my project "Quantum Tunnelling Without a Barrier: Phase-Sensitive Signatures in HHG". I tried to make these interactive but I couldn't get it to work, so if anybody knows how to do that in Julia pls let me know.

Two-colour switchover configurations

A switchover from œâ to 2œâ. Tunnelling without a barrier happens at the second ionisation event (green) when the components have equal amplitude. The total electric field shown here is given by

E ( t ) = E 1 cos ( œâ t ) + E 2 cos ( 2 œâ t + œï )

Harmonic variation of α vs laser intensity

Quantum-path phase coefficients over a scan of total laser intensity for a strongly bichromatic field. The varied quantity in the gif is the emitted harmonic. The branchings and cutoffs here correspond to partial coalescences between trajectories or to the termination of a trajectory when the harmonic cutoff is reached.

α surfaces for tunnelling without a barrier

Quantum-path phase coefficient surfaces over an independent field component grid. Again the animation shows the harmonic variation. The surface structure indicates the order of dependence of the phase sensitivity. I think it looks like dancing stingrays :)
